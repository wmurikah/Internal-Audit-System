Data Protection & Privacy

You will only see this once.

What we collect

Your name, email address, role, department, and affiliate assignment. Your activity within the audit system, including work paper actions, findings reviewed, and login events, is logged for audit trail and accountability purposes.

How we use it

Data is used solely for internal audit management: tracking audit observations and action plans, generating compliance and management reports, and maintaining accountability within the Hass Petroleum audit process. It is never shared with third parties for commercial purposes.

Your rights

You may request access to your personal data, correction of inaccurate data, or export of your data at any time. You may also raise a concern about how your data is handled. Requests are processed within 30 days in accordance with the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019.

Contact

Address data privacy concerns or requests to the Head of Internal Audit at audit@hasspetroleum.com. All data is stored in Google Cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. Passwords are hashed using HMAC-SHA256 and are never stored in plain text.
