Data Privacy Notice

What we collect: Your name, email, role, department, and affiliate assignment. Your activity within the audit system is logged for audit trail purposes.

How we use it: Data is used solely for internal audit management, tracking audit observations and action plans, generating compliance reports, and maintaining accountability within the Hass Petroleum audit process.

Data protection: All data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore with encryption at rest and in transit. Access is role-based and restricted to authorized personnel. Passwords are hashed using HMAC-SHA256.

Your rights: You may request access to your personal data, correction of inaccurate data, or raise concerns with the Head of Internal Audit at any time.

Data retention: Audit records are retained in accordance with Hass Petroleum's records retention policy and applicable regulatory requirements.
